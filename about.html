About Us

The Best Holidays Start Here!

Experience a serene escape at Kingsukh Guest House, nestled amidst the breathtaking landscapes of Purulia. Our guest house offers a perfect retreat with its panoramic views of Biharinath Hill, Baranti Hill, Susunia Hill, Joychandi Hill, Garhpanchkot, Baranti Dam, Maithon Dam, and the enchanting Panchat Dam.

At Kingsukh Guest House, we prioritize your comfort and relaxation. Our rooms are designed with care to ensure a restful stay, and our culinary offerings include a variety of delectable meals crafted to satisfy every palate. Enjoy your meals in our inviting dining area or take them to our lush garden oasis, where you can unwind and soak in the tranquility of your surroundings.

Explore the natural beauty and cultural richness of Purulia, where every corner holds a story waiting to be discovered. Whether you’re embarking on outdoor adventures, visiting local attractions, or simply savoring the peaceful ambiance of our guest house, we are committed to making your stay memorable.

Our attentive staff is dedicated to providing warm hospitality and ensuring that every aspect of your stay is enjoyable. Let us be your gateway to a unique and enchanting experience in Purulia, where nature and comfort come together in perfect harmony.
